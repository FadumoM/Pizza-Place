About Pizza Place



Connolly and his wife, Tina, moved to Plano in 1968 and opened Pizza Place in McKinney, Texas, in 1969. It became a local culinary landmark, with Connolly running the business for
more than 50 years before handing over the reins to his son, Scott, in January 2020.
